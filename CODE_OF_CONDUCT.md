# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## English

HermesX is a technical community for building governed agent runtime infrastructure. We expect contributors, maintainers, users, and reviewers to keep collaboration professional, evidence-based, and respectful.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, time zones, languages, and levels of experience.
- Discuss ideas, code, tradeoffs, and evidence instead of attacking people.
- Keep security-sensitive details out of public issues and follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- Prefer clear reproduction steps, concrete proposals, and actionable review comments.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information, credentials, exploit details, or confidential reports without permission.
- Deliberately misleading maintainers or users about security, licensing, or release status.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.

## 中文

HermesX 是一个围绕受治理 Agent 运行时基础设施的技术社区。我们希望贡献者、维护者、用户和评审者保持专业、基于证据、互相尊重的协作方式。

我们鼓励：

- 尊重不同背景、时区、语言和经验水平。
- 讨论想法、代码、取舍和证据，而不是攻击个人。
- 不在公开 issue 中披露安全敏感细节，并遵循 [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)。
- 提供清晰复现步骤、具体建议和可执行的评审意见。

不可接受：

- 骚扰、威胁、仇恨言论、人身攻击或持续扰乱协作。
- 未经允许公开个人信息、凭证、漏洞利用细节或保密报告。
- 故意误导维护者或用户对安全、许可证或发布状态的判断。

维护者可以编辑、隐藏、锁定或移除违反本准则的评论和 issue。严重或重复违规可能导致被移出项目协作空间。
