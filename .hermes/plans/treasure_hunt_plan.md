# Plan for Claimin' Buried Treasure on a Deserted Isle

**Ahoy, ye lucky landlubber!** Yer Captain Blackbeard has devised a foolproof treasure-huntin' strategy. Follow these orders to the letter, and ye'll be swimmin' in doubloons before the moon rises!

---

## 🎯 The Goal
Discover and claim the legendary buried treasure hidden somewhere on yonder deserted island—before any rival pirates, cursed spirits, or land sharks beat ye to it!

---

## 🗺️ Current Context & Assumptions

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Location** | Deserted island of unknown name (coordinates TBD) |
| **Intel** | We have a weathered treasure map with an X-marks-the-spot |
| **Crew** | Just ye and yer parrot for now |
| **Provisions** | Cutlass, shovel, rum (essential), compass, rope, lantern |
| **Threats** | Wild beasts, quicksand, booby traps, mutinous seagulls |

---

## 📜 Proposed Approach

### Phase 1: Reconnaissance & Landing
1. Sail close to the island and scout the coastline for safe anchorage
2. Look for landmarks mentioned on the map (crocodiles, distinctive trees, rock formations)
3. Establish a base camp near the beach—close to water, but on high ground

### Phase 2: Cracking the Clues
1. Study the map for riddles, symbols, or cryptic directions
2. Compare landmarks on the map to the actual terrain
3. Follow the "path of the crabs" or other nature-guided directions (pirates were clever!)

### Phase 3: The Hunt
1. Begin diggin' where the evidence points—X don't lie, matey!
2. Check for trap doors, hidden caves, or suspiciously placed skulls
3. Keep yer cutlass ready—some treasure guardians don't take kindly to visitors

### Phase 4: Claimin' the Spoils
1. Load the treasure into yer sea bags (don't forget the heavy gold—ye'll need strength!)
2. Mark the location for future reference (a true captain always has backup sites)
3. Return to the ship and set sail for port before rivals arrive

## ⚠️ Risks, Tradeoffs & Open Questions

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Treasure not at the X (common trick!) | Search surrounding area in expanding circles |
| Quicksand or sinkholes | Test ground with pole before steppin' |
| Hostile wildlife | Bring extra rum to bribe the animals |
| Map is faded/incomplete | Use intuition and pirate superstition |

### Open Questions:
- 🧭 **Is there a secondary clue?** Some maps require solving a riddle before diggin'
- 💀 **Are there cursed spirits?** May need to leave an offering (rum) at the site
- 🦜 **Is the parrot actually spy?** Some birds sell info to rival crews
